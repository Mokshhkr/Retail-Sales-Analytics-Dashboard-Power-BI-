# Retail-Sales-Analytics-Dashboard-Power-BI

## 📌 Project Overview  
This project focuses on analyzing retail e-commerce data to uncover insights related to sales performance, customer behavior, and operational efficiency. The objective was to build an interactive Power BI dashboard to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective  

- Analyze overall sales and revenue trends  
- Identify top-performing regions and products  
- Monitor delivery performance and delays  
- Understand customer payment preferences  
- Provide actionable insights to improve business performance  

---

## 🧠 Approach & Methodology  

- Imported and combined multiple datasets into Power BI  
- Performed data cleaning and transformation using Power Query  
- Created calculated columns and measures using DAX  
- Built interactive visualizations for better data exploration  
- Designed a clean and user-friendly dashboard layout  

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies  

- Power BI  
- Power Query  
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)  
- Excel (data source)  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features  

- **KPI Cards:** Total Revenue, Total Orders, Delivery Performance  
- **Sales Analysis:** Revenue trends over time  
- **Regional Analysis:** Performance across different locations  
- **Delivery Insights:** Delayed vs on-time orders  
- **Payment Analysis:** Distribution of payment methods  

---

## 🔍 Problem Statements & Solutions  

---

### 🔹 1. Sales Performance Analysis  

**Problem:**  
Understand overall revenue trends and identify growth patterns.

**Approach:**  
Analyzed time-based data to track revenue fluctuations and trends.

---

### 🔹 2. Regional Performance  

**Problem:**  
Identify which regions contribute the most to revenue.

**Approach:**  
Grouped data by region and compared sales performance across locations.

---

### 🔹 3. Delivery Performance  

**Problem:**  
Evaluate delivery efficiency and identify delays.

**Approach:**  
Compared delivery timelines and categorized orders as delayed or on-time.

---

### 🔹 4. Payment Method Analysis  

**Problem:**  
Understand customer payment preferences.

**Approach:**  
Analyzed distribution of different payment methods to identify dominant trends.

---

## 📈 Key Insights  

- Certain regions consistently contributed higher revenue, indicating strong market presence  
- A noticeable percentage of orders experienced delivery delays, impacting customer experience  
- Specific payment methods were preferred by customers, highlighting behavioral trends  
- Revenue trends showed periodic fluctuations, useful for forecasting and planning  

---

## 📊 Key Learnings  

- Hands-on experience with Power BI dashboard creation  
- Strong understanding of data visualization and storytelling  
- Improved skills in DAX and data modeling  
- Ability to translate business problems into visual insights  

---

## 🚀 Outcome  

Developed an interactive dashboard that enables stakeholders to monitor sales performance, identify inefficiencies, and make data-driven business decisions effectively.
